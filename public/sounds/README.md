# Audio Files Directory

Place your Wii-inspired sound files here:

- `wii-hover.mp3` - Short beep sound (~100-200ms)
- `wii-click.mp3` - Click/select sound (~200-300ms)
- `wii-whoosh.mp3` - Page transition sound (~300-500ms)

## Recommended Sound Sources

- [Freesound.org](https://freesound.org/) - Free audio effects
- [Zapsplat](https://www.zapsplat.com/) - Royalty-free sounds
- [BBC Sound Effects](https://sound-effects.bbcrewind.co.uk/) - Quality effects
- [The Sounds Resource](https://www.sounds-resource.com/wii/) - Authentic Wii sounds
- Record your own Wii UI sounds!

## Format Requirements

- Format: MP3 (recommended for browser compatibility)
- Alternative: WAV, OGG
- Duration: 100-500ms for best UX
- Volume: -6dB to -12dB to avoid harshness

## Usage

The application will automatically look for these sound files:
- `/sounds/wii-hover.mp3` on hover interactions
- `/sounds/wii-click.mp3` on click/tap
- `/sounds/wii-whoosh.mp3` on page transitions

Sound effects are optional - the app will work perfectly without them!
